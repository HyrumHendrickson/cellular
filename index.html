Cellular Automata Sandbox

Mathematical Research Environment
Instructions:
• Click colors to select, click grid to paint
• Use color names in rules (red, green, cyan, etc.)
• Neighborhoods: 3×3, 5×5, 7×7, 9×9, 11×11
• counts[size].colorName for color counts
• counts[size].total for all non-empty
• Return null for empty cells
• Step Back button reverses up to 100 steps
